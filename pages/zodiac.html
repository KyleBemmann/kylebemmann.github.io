The Zodiac

The concept of the zodiac originated in ancient Babylonia around the 5th century BCE. They divided the sky into 12 equal parts to track the Sun's path. The term itself comes from the ancient Greek phrase zōdiakos kyklos, meaning "circle of animals," because most of the constellations in this belt represent animals.

Aries

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac. It is known for its bold and ambitious personality. Aries dates are from March 21 to April 19. As a cardinal fire sign, Aries is characterized by passion and courage. The ruling planet is Mars. The symbol is the Ram. Aries rules the First House, which governs self-image and new beginnings. It is associated with the Greek myth of the Golden Fleece.

♈︎︎

Taurus

Taurus is the second sign of the zodiac, covering birthdays from approximately April 20 to May 20. It is an earth sign symbolized by the bull and ruled by the planet Venus. Those born under this sign are known for being grounded, dependable, and persistent, with a love for comfort and luxury.

♉︎︎

Gemini

The Gemini zodiac sign is the third astrological sign. It is represented by the twins, with an annual period typically between May 21 and June 20. Gemini is an air sign ruled by Mercury. Those born under this sign are known for intellectual curiosity, adaptability, and social nature.

♊︎︎

Cancer

Cancer is the fourth sign of the zodiac, a water sign symbolized by the Crab that typically governs the period from June 21 to July 22. Those born under this sign are known for being deeply emotional, intuitive, and nurturing individuals.

♋︎︎

Leo

Leo is the fifth sign of the zodiac, a fire sign ruled by the Sun. Represented by the Lion, Leos are known for their confident, vibrant, and natural leadership qualities. This sign typically governs the period from July 23 to August 22.

♌︎︎

Virgo

Virgo is the sixth sign of the zodiac, an earth sign symbolized by the Maiden and typically spanning from August 23 to September 22. Governed by the planet Mercury, Virgos are known for their analytical, practical, and detail-oriented approach to life.

♍︎︎

Libra

Libra (September 23–October 22) is the seventh sign of the zodiac, an air sign symbolized by the Scales. Ruled by the planet Venus, Libras are known for their charm, diplomacy, and deep appreciation for harmony, beauty, and fairness.

♎︎︎

Scorpio

Scorpio (October 23–November 21) is the eighth sign of the zodiac, a water sign symbolized by the Scorpion. Ruled by the planets Mars and Pluto, Scorpios are known for their intense passion, magnetism, and unwavering determination.

♏︎︎

Sagittarius

Sagittarius (November 22–December 21) is the ninth sign of the zodiac, a fire sign ruled by the planet Jupiter. Represented by the Archer (a centaur with a bow and arrow), Sagittarians are known for their love of freedom, adventure, and philosophical thinking.

♐︎︎

Capricorn

Capricorn is the tenth sign of the zodiac, an earth sign symbolized by the Sea-Goat. The annual period typically falls between December 22 and January 19. Governed by the planet Saturn, Capricorns are known for their ambitious, disciplined, and practical nature.

♑︎︎

Aquarius

Aquarius is the eleventh sign of the zodiac, an air sign represented by the Water Bearer that typically covers the period from January 20 to February 18. Governed by the planets Uranus and Saturn, Aquarians are known for their progressive thinking, independence, and humanitarian spirit.

♒︎︎

Pisces

Pisces is the twelfth and final astrological sign of the zodiac, typically covering the period from February 19 to March 20. It is a water sign, represented by two fish swimming in opposite directions, and known for its emotional depth, compassion, and creative nature.

♓︎︎
