A Day In My Life

I wake up early because I'm usually asleep by 8 or 9 o'clock, as that's the time my dad goes to bed and it's easier to stay in the routine. Anyway, every day is the same, mostly. Dad goes and plays hockey in the morning with his friends twice a week and when he's not around I have to entertain myself. My grandma is home with me when dad's gone, so I'm not completely alone. She likes making us nachos and pizza. Oh and she cleans a lot.

On Wednesday, dad and I get pizza at a local restaurant and on Saturdays we usually find some fun weekend activities to enjoy. Once a month we watch model rockets launch, we go downtown flint if they have anything going on, sometimes we watch BMX races, and anything else he finds on Facebook.

Other than that I'm chronically online. Sometimes I go grocery shopping with dad, and other times I just go for a car ride to get out of the house. We also go to estate sales every now and then. Sometimes we go out to eat at restaurants that are walking distance from home, and I like to order burgers with fries. When I take the fries home and reheat them I put cheese and onions on them. It's my favorite food.

I'm on antidepressants because my life sucks so I gained a little bit of weight, but it's not so bad. My life is boring, and after all the trauma I've endured, I've learned to accept how peaceful it is.
